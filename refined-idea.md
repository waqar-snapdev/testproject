**App Description**: A personal health dashboard that allows users to monitor and record their health vitals and symptoms. The application provides a timeline view to track health events and includes sections for managing medications and family health information.
**Target Users**: Individuals who want to track their personal health data and history.
**Core Features**:
1. Track personal health vitals.
2. Log and monitor symptoms.
3. View a timeline of health events.
4. Manage a list of medications.
5. Keep track of family members' health information.
**Technical Requirements**: None specified.
